About Us

My pa use to work as fisherman and he'd bring back salmon everyday after his shift for the family. We had salmon for breakfast, lunch, dinner. Salmon with eggs, salmon sandwiches, salmon pizza, salmon salad, salmon biscuits. Anything. You name it! My ma was a sharp as a blade and can whip up anything with salmon and we never grew tired of it.

One day, my ma made salmon cookies for me and my school buddies. They loved it. Loved it so much that we started to sell them to raise money for college. Folks ate em up!

My ma died years ago and we honor her, Patricia, with her famous Salmon Cookies. We grind up the salmon, and bake with butter, sugar, and little love. Just like ma use to.

Mission

To sell salmon cookies with the best quality ingredients and excellent customer service.
